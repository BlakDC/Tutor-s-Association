About VoteBright

VoteBright is a lightweight voting platform built for communities to nominate and select outstanding tutors. Submissions are approved by admins, votes are public, and data can be exported for records.

Mission

To empower communities to recognize great educators.

Vision

Transparent, fair and easy voting.

How it works

Register → Nominate → Vote. Admins validate nominees and manage users.
